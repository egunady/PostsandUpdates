Calculate Total

Add up the number of eggs in the rafts with the number of individual eggs to obtain your total.
Zika Timeline
Information courtesy of World Health Organization
1947 Scientists isolate the Zika virus in samples taken from a rhesus monkey in the Zika Forest of Uganda, Africa.
1948 The Zika virus is recovered from the mosquito Aedes africanus in the Zika forest.
1952 First human cases are confirmed in Uganda and the United Republic of Tanzania.
1969-1983 Zika spreads to India, Indonesia, Malaysia, and Pakistan, through mosquito vectors.
2007 First Zika outbreak in humans occurs in the Pacific Islands of Yap in the Federated States of Micronesia.
2008 A US scientist infected with Zika infects his wife, confirming that the virus can be sexually transmitted.
2013-2014 Outbreaks occur in the Pacific Islands of French Polynesia, Easter Island, the Cook Islands, and New Caledonia.
2015 Brazil declares a national public health emergency as number of microcephaly cases rises.
Feb 2016 The US reports two sexually transmitted cases of Zika virus.
Aug 2016 CDC issues travel warning over Miami Zika outbreak.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if I have Zika?

Symptoms include fever, skin rash, muscle or joint pain, headache, and conjunctivitis (pink eye). The only way to know for certain if you are infected is to be medically tested.

How can I protect myself from Zika?

Mosquitoes that spread Zika virus usually bite during the daytime. Protect yourself with insect repellent, and if going outdoors for extended periods, treat your clothing and gear with permethrin. Avoid traveling to areas where Zika is spreading.

How is Zika transmitted?

Mosquito bites

The Aedes species of mosquito (Ae. aegypti and Ae. albopictus) are primary vectors for transmitting Zika to humans. This species also spreads dengue and chikungunya viruses.

From mother to child

A fetus can obtain the Zika virus from its mother during pregnancy, leading to microcephaly. However, infants cannot get the virus through breastfeeding.

Through sex

Zika can be spread through sex even if the infected person does not show symptoms.

Zika can also be spread through blood transfusion and laboratory exposure.

Is there a cure?

Currently, there is no cure or vaccine for Zika.
